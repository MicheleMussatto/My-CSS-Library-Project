Hello

Welcome to Chillax.

Chillax is a cool, clean CSS library with built in parallaxing sections.  Simply swap in your own images and text and add and delete sections as needed.  Then insert our navbars, buttons, avatars and forms into our responsive 6-, 9- and 12-grid flexboxes and...  you're ready to go.

Like what you see so far?  Cool... keep scrolling, there's more chill stuff to come.  When you're ready to get started come back to this section and click on the buttons below to set up your parallaxing sections and navbar.  Or don't...  just pick the elements you want, it's all good.
